ABOUT ME

Coming from a small town and a small school, I've always dreamt of being part of a fast paced lifestyle, and a challenging workplace. In order to offset the fast pace I crave, my hobbies have revolved around a somewhat meditative process. While I play games and watch movies like anyone else, I also like to spend my extra time baking, exploring museums, and building miniature robot models from one of my favorite shows. Although my hobbies tend to paint me as a homebody, I love to get out and experience what the world has to offer. I've made it a point to travel somewhere new, at least once every year, and so far I have visited Tokyo and New York City (Plus a few beach trips between those). I am fueled by an urge to consistently learn and experience new things, always making sure I have a challenge to face.
